Your patients' data never leaves the building.

Most "AI-powered" health software sends your patients' records to someone else's cloud. Rootline takes the opposite path: the AI runs on hardware in your clinic.

On-premise AI

Speech recognition and clinical language models run locally on the appliance — no cloud AI processing of patient records.

Private by architecture

The system is reachable only over your clinic's secure private network. There is no public login page to attack.

Audited nightly

A built-in HIPAA sentinel scans every access log each night and flags anything that doesn't look like normal work.

Everything a PT clinic runs on, in one system.

Documentation that writes itself

Dictate or just talk with your patient — the ambient scribe drafts the SOAP note as the visit happens. You review and sign.

Denials prevented, not appealed

Every claim is checked against payer rules before submission. When a denial does happen, an appeal letter is drafted for you.

A billing agent that works nights

Overnight, the appliance checks claim statuses, identifies denials, drafts appeals, and has a digest waiting in the morning.

Scheduling & front desk

Appointments, recurring visits, insurance eligibility checks, and authorization tracking in one place.

Claims & clearinghouse

Electronic claims and real-time eligibility through the Office Ally clearinghouse, with CPT coding suggestions from your notes.

HIPAA Sentinel

Nightly rule-based audit scans with on-box AI triage. Compliance findings appear in a dashboard; real concerns alert you immediately.

Home health & car mode

A drive-safe mobile flow for home visits: patient briefing on the way, voice documentation after, hands mostly on the wheel.

Patient engagement

Home exercise adherence, attendance patterns, and outreach — so patients who are drifting get attention before they drop out.

Standards-based

HL7 FHIR interoperability, so your data is portable and connectable — not locked in.
