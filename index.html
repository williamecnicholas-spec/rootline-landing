01 · Private by architecture

Your patients' data never leaves the building.

Most "AI-powered" health software sends your patients' records to someone else's cloud. Rootline takes the opposite path: the AI runs on hardware inside your clinic.

On-premise AI

Speech recognition and clinical language models run locally on the appliance — no cloud AI processing of patient records.

No public login

The system is reachable only over your clinic's secure private network. There is no public door for attackers to knock on.

Audited nightly

A built-in HIPAA sentinel reviews every access log each night and flags anything that doesn't look like normal work.

02 · What it does

Everything a PT clinic runs on, in one system.

One appliance replaces the EMR, the scribe subscription, the billing service, and the compliance consultant.

01

Ambient documentation

Dictate or just talk with your patient — the scribe drafts the SOAP note as the visit happens. You review and sign.

02

Denial prevention

Every claim is checked against payer rules before submission. When a denial does happen, an appeal letter is drafted for you.

03

Overnight billing agent

While the clinic sleeps, the appliance checks claim statuses, identifies denials, drafts appeals, and leaves a digest for the morning.

04

Scheduling & front desk

Appointments, recurring visits, insurance eligibility checks, and authorization tracking in one place.

05

Claims & clearinghouse

Electronic claims and real-time eligibility through the Office Ally clearinghouse, with CPT coding suggestions from your notes.

06

HIPAA Sentinel

Nightly rule-based audit scans with on-box AI triage. Findings live in a dashboard; real concerns alert you immediately.

07

Home health & car mode

A drive-safe mobile flow for home visits: patient briefing on the way there, voice documentation on the way back.

08

Patient engagement

Home exercise adherence, attendance patterns, and outreach — so drifting patients get attention before they drop out.

09

Standards-based

HL7 FHIR interoperability, so your data stays portable and connectable — not locked in.
